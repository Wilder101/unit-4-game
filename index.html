CrystalsCollector Game

Instructions:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four cystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the given random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the given random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
